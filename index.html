Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a nutrition consultation?

You can book a consultation directly through our website by clicking "Find a Nutritionist" and selecting your preferred specialist. Alternatively, you can call our office at +1 (555) 123-4567 to schedule an appointment.

Are virtual consultations available?

Yes! We offer secure video consultations for all our services. After booking, you'll receive a Zoom link to connect with your nutritionist from the comfort of your home.

What's the difference between a dietitian and nutritionist?

Dietitians are clinically trained and licensed professionals who can diagnose eating disorders and design meal plans for medical conditions. Nutritionists focus more on general wellness and healthy eating habits. All our professionals are properly certified for their specialties.

Do you provide meal plans for specific conditions?

Absolutely. Our dietitians specialize in creating personalized meal plans for diabetes, heart health, digestive issues, food allergies, and other medical conditions. Just mention your needs when booking.

How often should I meet with a nutritionist?

We typically recommend weekly sessions for the first month, then bi-weekly as you establish new habits. Maintenance consultations are monthly or quarterly depending on your goals.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with many major insurance providers for medically necessary nutrition therapy. Please contact our office with your insurance details and we'll verify your coverage.
